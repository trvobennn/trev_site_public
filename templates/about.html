About Me

I was born in 1992 and have lived in the Tampa, FL area my whole life thus far.

I attended Wesley Chapel High and followed that with an AA diploma from Hillsborough Community College (2015).

I have held a number of jobs in the service sector and manufacturing over the past 10 years. Some of these were through temp agencies. I had gone through tech school and trained in welding as an expedient but found rather little work available in Central Florida for manufacturing or in welding or fabricating generally. That work was inconsistent and neither did I find it suitable as a long term prospect for a number of reasons.

As of 2021, I began learning Python and the basics of MS Azure. I believe the GitHub is a decent reflection of what I have learned. I have also read texts on Python, computer science more broadly, and dabbled in R.
